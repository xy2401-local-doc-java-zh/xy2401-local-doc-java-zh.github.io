JD Edwards

JD Edwards EnterpriseOne

Oracle's JD Edwards EnterpriseOne is a powerful, fully-integrated ERP software suite that provides more choice of databases and deployment options, including on-premise, private cloud, public cloud or hybrid cloud for maximized flexibility and low total cost of ownership. With over 80 application modules, end-user reporting, and personalization capabilities, JD Edwards EnterpriseOne combines business value, standards-based technology, and deep industry functionality to drive operational and business process modernization. Designed for the digital economy, JD Edwards provides mobility to move JD Edwards beyond the back office to the point of the business process and Internet of Things (IoT) integration to power real-time insight enabling faster responses to market opportunities.

JD Edwards World

Oracle's JD Edwards World solution enables collaboration both within and outside your business network. Your lines of business work together sharing information, improving operational performance, trimming costs, and driving profitable growth. Companies of all sizes from around the globe trust their business to run on JD Edwards World because of its low total cost of ownership, stability, and breadth of industry products.
