Description of the illustration jsgct_dt_006_prm_gn_sz.png

This figure consists of a row of six rectangles. These rectangles are labeled in this order.

  1. Eden

  2. Survivor

  3. Survivor

  4. Virtual

  5. Old

  6. Virtual

Groups of rectangles are labeled as follows:

  • Total size: Rectangles 1 through 6.

  • Committed vs. Virtual: Committed consists of rectangles 1 through 3 and 5; virtual consists of rectangles 4 and 6.

  • Old to Young ratio: Old consists of rectangles 1 through 4; young consists of rectangles 5 and 6.

  • Eden to Survivor space ratio: Eden is rectangle 1; survivor space is rectangle 2.
