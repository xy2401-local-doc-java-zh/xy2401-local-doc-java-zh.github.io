Core Session Manager Documentation

Oracle Communications Core Session Manager is an agile session core for supporting Voice over LTE (VoLTE), Rich Communications Suite (RCS), fixed-network modernization, Over-the-top (OTT) service integration and more. Oracle Communications Core Session Manager is designed from the ground up to be 100% virtualized and in fully orchestrated aligned with NFV framework.

Search tips
How do I narrow my search results?

After you submit your search query:

  1. On the Refine Search results page, select one or more categories of products or services from the left sidebar.
  2. Then for a category with search results, click Select to choose your product and release filters, and then click OK.

For search queries that contain multiple words, surround the query with quotes, and then resubmit your query. Example: "database cloud service"

Navigation tips
How do I find the documentation for my product or service?

From the home page, click the technology categories for your products or services. If you do not know which category to pick, try the following features:

  • Click the Find a product tab and search for your product or service.
  • Click Browse All Products & Services at the bottom of the home page to view an alphabetical listing of products and services.
