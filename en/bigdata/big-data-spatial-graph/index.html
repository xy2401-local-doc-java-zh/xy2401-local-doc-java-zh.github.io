Oracle Big Data Spatial and Graph

Oracle Big Data Spatial and Graph provides advanced spatial analytic capabilities and graph database for Big Data platforms. It includes a distributed property graph database with many parallel, in-memory analytics to analyze relationships in social media and other big data graphs; and spatial services to evaluate spatial relationships, enrich big data with real-world locations and boundaries, and process and visualize geospatial map data and imagery.
