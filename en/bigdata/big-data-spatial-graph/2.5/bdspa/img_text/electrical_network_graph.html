Description of the illustration for an electrical network graph

The electrical network graph contains icons for devices (squares), connections (triangles), and switches (circles), with lines showing connections between the objects. At the top is the Source device. It is connected to two connections, and connections continue downward between various objects. All of the connecting lines have arrowheads at both ends.
