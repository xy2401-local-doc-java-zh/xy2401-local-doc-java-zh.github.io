Shows register for email notifications screen where you enter the email address to receive notifications, subscribe or unsubscribe and choose the type of collection notifications to receive. Available collection notifications are:

  • New Collection without comparisons

  • Collections regressed with warnings

  • Collections that improved with passes

  • Collection regressed with failures

  • ORAchk CM Tablespace notifications where the space in database falls below 100 MB

You can test the email settings and then submit.
